["Gay of Thrones"](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Q8PD_GvWrT0&list=PL77kq7xMnzVMN-4l8bh2OUGUofydgxU-q) is a web series on FunnyorDie where a hairdresser, Jonathon, recaps ["Game of Thrones"](http://www.hbo.com/game-of-thrones) episodes with a client, who is usually another fellow comedian or actor, every week throughout a season. Each video from the series contains clips from the show "Game of Thrones," sound bites from other shows and copyrighted music.
