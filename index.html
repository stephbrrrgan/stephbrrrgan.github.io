⚡ Welcome to the University of Pittsburgh's Quidditch Team! ⚡

For seven years, Quidditch has been an important aspect in the lives of a unique group of Pitt students. The bonds made over muddy practices, tournament weekends, and meals at Market have solidified many friendships since its creation. If you've ever been on a campus tour with one of the infamous Pitt Pathfinders, you've probably already heard them briefly mention the team. Whatever you might have heard about us, there's a lot more to "muggle" Quidditch than just some college students running around on brooms.

Believe it or not, universities and communities around the country have formed teams. Originating in Vermont, "muggle" Quidditch has spread across the country, eventually forming a United States Quidditch league. During a typical season, these teams verse one another in tournaments hosted by different universities. These tournaments lead up to regional championships. Some regions include the Mid-Atlantic, Southwest, and Great Lakes, which is where the Pitt team competes every year. Check out the rest of the site to learn more!
